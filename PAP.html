How This Works?

Minimum CTC

If your salary exceeds the CTC of 5,00,000/-, your monthly payments of PAP will start.

1 Year

If you don't get a job offer within 1 year of the course, you pay nothing for your learning at CourseHub.

Enforcement

In the event you are not working or if your income drops below the CTC mentioned in the pay after placement Agreement, the monthly payments pause.

Course Fee

These only take effect once you start earning above the PAP threshold amount for your course.

  • Average CTC: > 8Lpa
  • Monthly Payable: 2,500
  • Training Tenure: 4 Months
  • Total Payable Fee: 1,50,000

CTC (cost to company) is defined as the total gross income earned, including but not limited to variable pay, compensations, and Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP). Find out more in the FAQ section.

Pay After Placement (PAP) Agreement

PAP Agreement is a legal contract that makes education at CourseHub outcome-based. It is not an education loan, as you do not have to pay any interest & you do not require any collaterals. If you do not get placed within 1 year of course completion, your learning with CourseHub is completely free.
